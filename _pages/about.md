* I am a Research Scientist at Meta Superintelligence Labs (MSL). Previously I was at the GenAI organization and before that I worked at FAIR. Prior joining Meta, I was a Senior Applied Scientist at Microsoft Cloud and AI from 2018 to 2020. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at [Michigan State University](https://msu.edu) in 2018, where I was a member of the [Computer Vision Lab](http://cvlab.cse.msu.edu) lead by Professor [Xiaoming Liu](https://cse.msu.edu/~liuxm/index2.html). In 2013, I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from [Wuhan University](https://en.whu.edu.cn).

* Since 2021, my main research focus is on video generation. Please check my [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=FAEzhskAAAAJ&hl=en) for a list of publications.
